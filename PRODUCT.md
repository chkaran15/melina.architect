# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective clients, collaborators, and hiring partners evaluating a premium digital portfolio. They arrive to understand taste, technical depth, interaction craft, and whether the maker can translate ambitious ideas into polished production work.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Melina Architect as a high-craft digital portfolio and landing page. Success means the page feels memorable, refined, technically sharp, and easy to scan, while creating enough confidence for visitors to explore work or start a conversation.

## Brand Personality

Editorial, precise, tactile.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page patterns, flat skills grids, excessive glass effects, loud rainbow gradients, and redesigns that erase the current white-canvas, black-ink, clay-accent identity.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the existing visual identity before extending it.
- Let typography, spacing, and motion carry the premium feeling.
- Show technical capability through composed systems, not clutter.
- Keep animation subtle, readable, and respectful of reduced motion.
- Build reusable primitives that future sections can share.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target accessible contrast, semantic sections and headings, keyboard-safe interactions, and reduced-motion fallbacks for animated UI.
